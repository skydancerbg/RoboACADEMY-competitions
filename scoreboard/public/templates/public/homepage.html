About the Project

ROBO STEAM ACADEMY is an Erasmus+ KA220-VET project (Grant KA220-VET-7CF4F308) bringing together partner institutions from Bulgaria, Slovakia, and Romania to advance robotics and STEM education in vocational training.

The competition platform enables students to build, program, and test autonomous robots in structured challenges. Using Raspberry Pi Pico W microcontrollers and our open-source MicroPython competition library, teams compete in categories ranging from automated lap timing to judged object manipulation tasks.

The project runs from October 2024 to September 2027 with a total budget of €400,000 and is developed as part of WP3 — Advanced Robo STEM Games and Jury.
